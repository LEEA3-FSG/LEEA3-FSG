Présentation

The Bachelor's degree in Electronics, Electrotechnics, and Automation (EEA) is a versatile program that prepares students to master fundamental and advanced concepts in electronics, automated systems, and energy technologies. This curriculum combines theory and practice, covering areas such as circuit design, embedded systems programming, electrical networks, and industrial automation. It provides a strong scientific and technical foundation, paving the way for diverse career opportunities in engineering, research, and technological innovation.
